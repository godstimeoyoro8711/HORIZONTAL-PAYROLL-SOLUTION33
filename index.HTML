Remote Assistant Role

Job Description

As a Remote Assistant at Horizon Payroll Solutions, you'll be responsible for providing administrative support to our clients, helping them manage their payroll processes efficiently and accurately.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Process payroll data entry and verification
  • Assist with basic accounting and bookkeeping tasks
  • Respond to client inquiries via email and phone
  • Maintain accurate records and documentation
  • Generate and review reports for accuracy
  • Collaborate with team members to ensure timely processing
  • Identify and resolve discrepancies in payroll data
  • Adhere to security protocols and confidentiality standards

Applicant Requirements

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent (Bachelor's degree preferred)
  • 1+ years of administrative or data entry experience
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite (especially Excel)
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Technical Requirements

  • Reliable computer with updated operating system
  • High-speed internet connection
  • Quiet workspace free from distractions
  • Webcam and microphone for virtual meetings
  • Ability to install required software applications
